Who is Abdie Mohamed?

Abdie Mohamed is a passionate speaker and career coach who helps individuals navigate the ever-changing world of tech and personal growth. Born and raised in San Diego, California, Abdie became the first in his family to pursue a career in technology, blazing a trail that inspired his younger siblings to follow suit and launch their own paths in the industry. Giving back to his community and maintaining strong relationships with family are core values that guide everything he does. With over six years of experience in cybersecurity, cloud, and leadership, Abdie has built a reputation for translating complex technical ideas into real-world guidance that empowers people to take bold action in their careers and lives. He is passionate about developing leaders and teaching them how to lead through effective communication. Abdie’s educational journey began at Grossmont College, where he studied Economics and developed a foundation in critical thinking and problem-solving. He later earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Management Information Systems from San Diego State University and a Master’s Degree in Cybersecurity Management and Policy from the University of Maryland Global Campus.

In addition to his degrees, he holds multiple industry certifications that reflect his technical depth and strategic mindset, which he brings to every speaking engagement and coaching session. Beyond keynotes and workshops, Abdie has also served as an MC for hackathons designed for aspiring young tech professionals, helping to curate engaging and inclusive experiences for the next generation of innovators. His presence on stage is marked by energy, authenticity, and a clear passion for uplifting others. He is especially driven to support first-generation professionals, career changers, and those breaking into tech, using his own story as proof that with the right tools and guidance, anyone can thrive. Above all, he inspires people to take action so they can realize their hopes and dreams. At the core of his mission is a belief that everyone deserves access to knowledge, support, and community. Whether he is coaching a young professional or energizing a crowd of students, Abdie’s goal is always the same. He helps people show up with purpose, communicate with clarity, and take the next step forward with confidence while honoring the importance of strong relationships and giving back.
