Inspiring Growth Through Leadership, Purpose, and Bold Action

Abdie Mohamed is a passionate speaker and career coach who helps people break through barriers, build confidence, and step into their full potential. With a background in cybersecurity, cloud technology, and leadership, Abdie combines real-world experience with practical advice to motivate audiences to take bold, meaningful steps toward their goals. As the first in his family to pursue a career in tech, Abdie’s story and message connect deeply with anyone looking to create a purposeful and impactful career.

A Speaker Who Sparks Change

Abdie’s journey is one of paving new paths and personal growth. Born and raised in San Diego, California, he was the first in his family to start a career in technology which inspired his younger siblings and community to do the same.
Known for his energetic and authentic style Abdie creates spaces where people feel encouraged to lead through clear communication and intentional action. His talks focus on leadership skills such as communication, personal development, and overcoming challenges with resilience and confidence. Audiences walk away inspired, equipped, and ready to lead themselves and others forward.
