About Me

Abdie Mohamed is a dynamic speaker and career coach dedicated to helping individuals unlock their full potential in tech and beyond. With a professional background in cybersecurity, cloud engineering, and leadership, he brings a rare blend of technical expertise and human-centered coaching to every stage, classroom, or 1-on-1 session. From guiding students toward their first internship to helping professionals navigate career transitions, Abdie focuses on practical strategies, confidence-building, and effective communication.

As the first in his family to pursue a career in tech, Abdie understands the challenges and opportunities that come with forging your own path. He has delivered talks at universities, tech conferences, and community events, translating complex topics like career clarity, personal branding, and communication into actionable insights. His mission is simple: to help people show up with purpose, speak with power, and step boldly into the next phase of their careers
